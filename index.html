How to play

👊

Punch

Click anywhere on the screen to throw a punch. Every hit is worth +250 pts and drains the Bull's health.

🛡️

Block

When the red fist comes at your face, hold the BLOCK bar (or SPACE) to raise your guard. A perfect block earns a bonus!

💀

Knock out

Empty the red bar before he empties yours. Send the Bull to the ground and own the alley.

About the game

BULLFUN is a tribute to the classic first-person brawlers from the golden age of Flash — now with the horns on. No downloads, no sign-up: open it, punch. Works on desktop and mobile.
