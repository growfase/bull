How to play

👊

Punch

Click anywhere on the screen to throw a punch. Every hit is worth +250 pts and drains Ansem's health.

🛡️

Block

When the red fist comes at your face, hold the BLOCK bar (or SPACE) to raise your guard. A perfect block earns a bonus!

💀

Knock out

Empty the red bar before he empties yours. Drop Ansem to the ground and unlock the damn airdrop.

About the game

BULLFUN is a tribute to the classic first-person brawlers from the golden age of Flash. Ansem has been gatekeeping the airdrop we've all been waiting for, so put the horns on, walk into the alley and take it back. Free to play, no sign-up, works on desktop and mobile. Your score only counts for the ranking if you connect your Solana wallet.
